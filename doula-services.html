Services

As a postpartum doula, my focus is on the new mother and infant from birth up to 16 weeks. I provide educational and emotional support not only to the mother, but also to the entire family. I will work with you to create a customized postpartum plan that will focus on your specific requirements and new parenting expectations.

Although I am based in North Carolina, I will travel nationally for your postpartum and newborn coaching needs. Below are some of the services that I frequently offer to clients, but each family is unique and I will strive to create the ideal plan for you. Please contact me for a no obligation, free consultation where we can discuss your postpartum care needs. I look forward to meeting you!

- Cindy MacKinnon

  • Newborn care education and instruction
  • Greater breastfeeding success
  • Better recovery time for mother
  • Ease stress and anxiety of new mother and family
  • Empower mothers with evidence-based and experiential information

Services offered

Newborn education

  • Breastfeeding support and education
    • Guidance with pumping and breast milk storage information
  • Colic and reflux
  • Circumcision care
  • Swaddling and soothing techniques
  • Newborn bathing information
  • Umbilical cord care
  • Aid with understanding typical newborn characteristics week-by-week
    • Physical characteristics
    • Identifying infant's cues for feeding, sleeping, etc.
    • Educate mother and father on infant's growth patterns
  • Babywearing and slings
  • Sterilization of bottles
  • Understanding formula information and preparation
  • Organization of home to create an effective space
    • Guidance purchasing newborn items

Emotional and physical support

  • Ensure the overall physical and emotional well-being of the new mother
    • Non-judgmental listening and open dialogue of wants, needs, and expectations
    • Help new mother to hone her instincts
    • Watch for early signs of postpartum depression
    • Keep mother nourished and hydrated
    • Allow new mother to rest when needed
  • Light housekeeping/laundry
  • Daytime and overnight care available
    • Daytime care: Minimum of 3 hours
    • Overnight care: Stay for 8-12 hours
  • Accompany to doctors visits and errands
  • Meal preparation
  • Family support and guidance
    • Sibling adjustment
    • Assist father with understanding new mother's emotional needs
    • Help father develop his important role in caring for the new infant
    • Encourage a sense of teamwork between the parents
