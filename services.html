Services

As a postpartum doula, my focus is on the new mother and infant from birth up to 16 weeks. I provide educational and emotional support not only to the mother, but also to the entire family. I will work with you to create a customized postpartum plan that will focus on your specific requirements and new parenting expectations. Although I am based in North Carolina, I will travel nationally for your postpartum doula and newborn coaching needs. Each family is unique and I will strive to create the ideal plan for you. Please contact me for a no obligation, free consultation where we can discuss your postpartum care needs. I look forward to meeting you!
